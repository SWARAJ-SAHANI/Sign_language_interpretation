How It Works

Our application uses Mediapipe to capture hand landmarks in real time. These landmarks are normalized and processed by our machine learning model, which interprets the signs and displays them on screen. The system then provides audio feedback by speaking the recognized sign aloud.

Users can also add custom signs, making the platform adaptable to personal vocabularies.
